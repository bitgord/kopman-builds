About Kopman Build

Kopman Build has been in business since 1999 and we have created beautiful indoor and outdoor spaces for many families for years to come. We work with designers and engineers as well as directly with home-owners on any scale of residential work. We pride ourselves on our punctuality, cleanliness and our attention to detail. We are often told by our clients how clean and organized their homes are and their ease in communicating with our staff. We are here to ensure our clients goals and expectations are reviewed daily to ensure progress and quality of work.
